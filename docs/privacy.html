GrokCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

GrokCast ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide weather forecasts and AI-powered features:

  • Location Data: Precise and approximate location (latitude/longitude) to deliver accurate local weather, forecasts, radar, and alerts. This is collected when the app is in use and, with your permission, in the background.
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app (e.g., which features you use, errors encountered).
  • Weather Queries & Context: When you use Grok AI features, we send relevant weather data (current conditions, forecast) along with your question to xAI to generate helpful responses.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time weather forecasts, radar, alerts, and location-based features.
  • To power AI responses through xAI's Grok models (weather context is shared only when you actively use the AI features).
  • To improve the app and fix bugs.

Information We Share

We share information with the following third parties:

  • xAI: Your weather context and questions are sent to xAI when using Grok AI features.
  • Apple: We use device location services (for local forecasts and background alerts) and may reference public NWS station data.
  • Mapbox: Used for the interactive radar map (if enabled).
  • Other service providers: For analytics and crash reporting (if any).

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

Location data is used in real-time and is not stored on our servers. Weather context sent to xAI is processed to generate responses and is subject to xAI's data practices.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in your device Settings.
  • You can stop using the AI features if you do not want weather context shared with xAI.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please visit our Support page or open an issue on GitHub.
