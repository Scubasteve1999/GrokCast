DESCRIPTION (4000 char max)
------------------------------------------------------------

GrokCast is a next-generation weather app powered by AI. Get hyper-local forecasts, real-time radar, and intelligent insights that help you plan your day — not just check the temperature.

WHAT MAKES GROKCAST DIFFERENT

- GrokCast Score — A single 0-100 number that tells you how good it is to be outside right now, factoring in temperature, humidity, UV, wind, and precipitation.

- AI Daily Briefing — Wake up to a personalized weather summary written by Grok AI. Not a template — an actual briefing that tells you what to wear, when to leave, and what to watch for.

- Storm Spotter — Point your camera at the sky and get an AI-powered analysis of cloud formations and severe weather potential.

- Minutecast — Know exactly when rain starts and stops in the next 2 hours down to the minute.

PROFESSIONAL-GRADE DATA

- Open-Meteo primary forecasts with NWS observations and alerts for US locations
- Live NEXRAD radar (IEM) and international coverage via RainViewer
- Forecast radar with animated precipitation outlook (OWM + Xweather)
- Air quality index, pollen levels, and UV tracking

SEVERE WEATHER ALERTS

- Critical interruption alerts for life-threatening events (tornado warnings, hurricane warnings) that break through Do Not Disturb
- Rich lock screen notifications with affected area, expiry countdown, and NWS instructions
- One tap to view on radar or read full alert details

WIDGETS & COMPLICATIONS

- Small, Medium, and Large home screen widgets with daily forecast
- Lock Screen widgets (circular gauge, rectangular, inline)
- Apple Watch complications with temperature range gauge and AI brief
- Live Activity with GrokCast Score on your Lock Screen

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

- No account required for core features
- Location used only for weather — never tracked or stored
- API keys stored in iOS Keychain, never in plain text

GrokCast Pro unlocks Grok AI without your own API key, forecast radar, Live Activity, unlimited saved locations, and AI morning briefings.
