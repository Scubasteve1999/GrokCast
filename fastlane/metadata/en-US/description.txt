SpotterCast is a modern, dark-themed weather app that delivers accurate forecasts with optional AI weather intelligence.

See stunning current conditions, an interactive radar map, hourly and 10-day forecasts, and get contextual AI insights about the weather. Ask about what to wear, whether it's good for hiking, or if storms are developing.


Features:
• Beautiful hero view with live conditions
• Detailed hourly and 10-day forecast
• Interactive radar with animation and overlays
• Severe weather alerts from the National Weather Service
• AI chat for weather analysis and advice
• Storm Spotter photo analysis for developing weather
• Home screen and Lock Screen widgets
• Location support and background alerts
• Clean, professional dark design

Built for people who want more than just basic weather data.
